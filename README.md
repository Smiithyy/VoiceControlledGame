# VoiceControlledGame
In this exciting island defense game, you are tasked with defending your island from waves of attacking pirate ships. Using cutting-edge voice recognition, you control a powerful cannon to fend off the invaders. Aim, fire, and reload your cannon entirely through voice commands, making for a unique and immersive experience. As you progress, the pirate ships grow more aggressive, and the waves become increasingly difficult, challenging your reflexes and strategy. Created in Unity using C#, this game combines dynamic gameplay with intuitive voice controls to keep you on your toes. How long can you survive the pirate assault?
